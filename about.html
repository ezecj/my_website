Who We Are

Welcome to DEVICKLIN FARMS ‚Äî a mixed agriculture paradise where tradition meets modern farming. Devicklin Farms is a registered agricultural entity in Nigeria, specializing in animal husbandry and crop farming with a comprehensive approach to breeding, production, and distribution of poultry products. Our activities include: - Poultry farming: breeding and processing commercial broilers for meat, producing high-quality pullets and broilers, breeding layers for egg production, and distributing fresh eggs. - Aquaculture: catfish breeding and rearing. - Crop farming: cultivating a range of crops to support our animal feed production and enhance food security. We prioritize natural feeds and practices, avoiding additives and artificial substances, to ensure healthy animals and quality products. We produce our own poultry feeds, ensuring nutritional needs are met through high-quality, natural ingredients. Our product line includes value-added poultry products like frozen chicken, sausages, dried packaged chicken meats, etc. These products cater for the needs of fast foods , restaurants, hotel, catering services and supermarkets across Nigeria. We also support the agricultural sector with vaccine supply, laboratory services, and training programs for farmers.
